## Structured Learning Programs

Courses provide the scaffolded progression that self-study cannot always offer. The best AI courses combine conceptual explanations with hands-on coding exercises, building both understanding and practical skill.

<KeyTakeaway>
Courses work best when you apply what you learn immediately. Choose a course that is one level above your current comfort zone, and pair it with a real project where you can apply each module's concepts. The combination of structured learning and practical application accelerates fluency faster than either approach alone.
</KeyTakeaway>
